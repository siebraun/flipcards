Yule and Winter Solstice Drinks

Wassail

To 6-8 cups of Apple Cider add:

⛤ 2 sliced Oranges (for joy and welcoming back the sun)

⛤ 2 sliced Apples (for energy)

⛤ a few cloves (for prosperity)

⛤ a pinch of Nutmeg (for good luck)

⛤ a bit of Allspice (for vitality)

⛤ a few slices of fresh ginger (for energy and potency)

⛤ 1/2 cup of Honey(for blessings and attraction)

Milk and Honey

To every 8oz (1C./240mL) of milk (goddess energy, love, nurturing, spirituality), add

⛤ 1 overflowing tablespoon (15mL) of honey (happiness, healing, purification, wisdom, sweetness, spirituality).

Pom Punch

To 3 cups equal parts Pomegranate and Cranberry juice add:

⛤ 1 sliced Orange (for joy)

⛤ 10 whole cloves (for prosperity)

⛤ 2 star anise (for protection)

⛤ 1 cinnamon stick, broken in half (for prosperity)

⛤ 5 black peppercorns, lightly cracked (to banish negativity)

⛤ 1" piece of fresh ginger, peeled and sliced into 1/4" chunks (for healing)

Honey and Lemon

To 1 1/4 cup water add:

⛤ 1/3 cup lemon juice (for sun magic and happiness)

⛤ 1/4 cup honey (rejuvination)

⛤ tiny pinch of salt

Turmeric and Ginger Tea

To 1 cup of Water add:

⛤ 1/4 teaspoon ground turmeric (for anti-inflammation)

⛤ 1/4 teaspoon ground ginger (for prosperity)

⛤ Splash of milk or soy milk (for nurturing and spirituality)

⛤ maple syrup, to taste (for love and longevity)
